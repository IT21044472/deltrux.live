F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Can your solution integrate with existing security infrastructure like SIEMs or firewalls?

Yes, our product is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing security tools, including SIEMs, firewalls, and other network monitoring systems. This ensures that threat data can be shared and managed centrally, improving the overall security posture.

How do you handle encrypted data or communication in your detection process?

While we do not decrypt encrypted communications, our product analyzes the behavior and metadata around encrypted sessions, such as unusual encryption attempts, traffic patterns, and access to sensitive data, to identify potential insider threats.

How does your system adapt to evolving insider threats and new attack vectors?

Our machine learning models continuously learn from new data, adapting to changes in user behavior and evolving attack patterns. Regular updates and patches also ensure the system stays current with the latest security threats and insider attack vectors.

Can I use this product in a cloud-based or hybrid environment?

Absolutely. Our product is compatible with both on-premises and cloud-based environments, allowing it to monitor hybrid infrastructures. This ensures consistent protection across your entire network, regardless of where the data resides.

How does your system detect lateral movement within a network?

We analyze user and system behavior to identify lateral movement, such as unusual attempts to access different systems or elevate privileges. By monitoring patterns across devices and network segments, we can detect and flag potentially malicious activities.

How can your product help with regulatory compliance for industries like finance or healthcare?

Our solution provides detailed audit logs and reporting features, which are essential for meeting regulatory requirements like GDPR, HIPAA, or PCI-DSS. It helps organizations demonstrate compliance by monitoring user activity and providing forensic evidence in case of security incidents.
